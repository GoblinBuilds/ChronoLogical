Welcome to the Tutorial

Game Mechanics

Player starts the game with one locked card. Arrange the question card chronologically in the timeline.

  • Question: A question is displayed as a card on top of the screen.

  • Place at index: Choose the desired position to put a card at by input an index.
    • Index 0 = before the first card
    • Index 1 = after the first card

  • Timeline: The white line separating the unlocked cards from locked cards.

  • Unlocked card: Displayed on top of the timeline. Unlocked cards will be lost when player answered incorrectly.

  • Locked card: Displayed under the timeline. Locked cards will not be lost when player answered incorrectly.

Buttons

  • Place: Place a card on the chosen index.

  • Lock: Lock the unlocked cards. This action decrease a 'lock-ins'. Player gets 3 lock-ins per game.

  • End Game: End the game session and go back to homepage.

Statistics

  • Stage: After placing 10 correct cards, player can choose to continue playing the game.

  • Lock-ins: Use to lock the unlocked cards. Player gets 3 lock-ins per game. When all lock-ins are used, it's a gameover and the game returns to homepage.

  • Score: Total correct question answered.
